Ways to resolve your dispute

  • Self resolution can be the fastest and cheapest way to resolve disputes.
  • Assisted resolution may involve mean conciliation or mediation. It can save time and money for you and the community.
  • Formal resolution may mean going to a court or tribunal. It costs time and money but will give you a result that can be enforced.

Body corporate disputes

Formal resolution of many disputes between neighbours in a body corporate can only be dealt with through the Office of the Commissioner for Body Corporate and Community Management.
